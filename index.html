Trailhead

Conceptual logo, app design and micro interaction animations for Trailhead. Trailhead is a service for users to find and book campsites throughout North America.

Target audience:

  • Campers and hikers interested in locating, booking, and rating camping locations throughout America, ages 27-47
Deliverables:
  • An animated logo for use in social media, promotional videos, website/app banner.
  • A website and mobile app for e/commerce.
  • Clear brand identity used through visual iconography, color palette, typography. Must be small enough to work on mobile devices, large enough to work in video. Looping, five to ten seconds. Must work on light and dark backgrounds.
Look & Feel:
  • Contemporary yet cozy
